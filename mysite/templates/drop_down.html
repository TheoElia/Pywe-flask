Welcome To The Course


Having a conversation with your computer might sound like
the script for a science fiction movie, but in this modern age with the rise of apple's Siri and other interactive software, perhaps you really don't find talking to your computer a fiction anymore. But to talk to your computer, you will discover the need to use a particular programming language to achieve that and since you are visiting this page, it's assumed you are interested in using Python for that. Python is a powerful programing language and very easy to learn, one major reason being that it's syntax is just like our everyday English language. You are welcomed. By the end of the course, you should be able to make simple calculators for anything, plot graphs as a way of visualising data, build python powered websites and more.
